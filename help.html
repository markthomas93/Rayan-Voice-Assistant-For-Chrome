Chrome Voice Assistant

Commands

  • For Google Search - Say "search" + anything

  • For Forwarding the tab - Say "forward"

  • For Backwarding the tab - Say "backward"

  • For Opening a new tab - Say "open new tab"

  • For Reloading the tab - Say "reload"

  • For Wikipedia search - Say "wikipedia" + anything

  • For Saavn search - Say "song" + anything

  • For Google Maos search - Say "maps" + anything

  • For Google Images search - Say "images of" or "photos of" + anything

  • For Scroll up - Say "scroll up

  • For Scroll down - Say "scroll down"

  • For Closing the opened tab - Say "close"

  • For Youtube search - Say "youtube" + anything

    • You can also use shortcut to execute commads- Ctrl+Shift+0


    This extension is in development phase and will improve in future versions. So look forward for future updates.
